Key Takeaways

This mentorship program will be divided into various phases. This program will run for three months until you get accepted and will resume again next year if you don't get accepted in the current year.

Introduction

Understand what are the basic factors on which universities shortlist your profile

Research

In this phase we will assign you topics on the basis of your research interests and help you to complete the research in a stipulated time and publish research paper at a good international conference

GRE and TOEFL prepration

We will give you resources and all the mentorship needed to prepare for these exams and will help you till you fare well on these.

Statement of purpose prepration

In this step we will help you prepare a statement of purpose on the basis of your research experience

Applying

In this phase we will help you shortlist a list of universities in which you should apply and also discuss some tricks which might help to increase your chances of selection
